Kat Hoddinott

My name is Kat Hoddinott. I am 19 years old. I was born in London, Ontario. I am currently enrolled at Fanshawe College for Interactive Media design. I was originally more interested in the design side of the program but I've found coding to be quite interesting as well.
My hobbies include photography, videography, playing music and hanging out with my pet rabbit.
I have worked a variety of jobs but none yet in a field related to my program.
I would say that my personal philosophy is "Life by the yard is hard but life by the inch is a cinch". I treat my work the same way. Instead of looking at a project as one big task, I break it down piece by piece to get it done.
